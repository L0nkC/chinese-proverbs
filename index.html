About This Collection

🎋 Chinese Proverbs (谚语)

Chinese proverbs are concise expressions of wisdom passed down through generations. They reflect the values, philosophy, and life experiences of Chinese culture.

🗣️ Cantonese Proverbs (粤语谚语)

Cantonese proverbs are unique to the Cantonese-speaking regions of Southern China, particularly Guangdong and Hong Kong. They are known for their vivid imagery and humor.

📝 Pinyin & Jyutping

Mandarin proverbs include Pinyin romanization. Cantonese proverbs use Jyutping for pronunciation guidance.
