## 🏮 About

A beautifully designed website featuring over 250 Chinese proverbs (谚语, *yànyǔ*), presented in both Mandarin Chinese and English. The collection includes:

- **Mandarin Proverbs** with Pinyin pronunciation
- **Cantonese Proverbs** (粤语谚语) with Jyutping romanization
- **Categorized by theme**: Wisdom, Learning, Perseverance, Friendship, Life

The design draws inspiration from ancient Chinese aesthetics, featuring traditional colors like vermilion red, imperial gold, and rice paper beige.

## 🎨 Design Philosophy

The website embraces traditional Chinese aesthetics:

| Element | Color | Hex | Meaning |
|---------|-------|-----|---------|
| Vermilion (朱砂) | Imperial Red | `#C73E1D` | Luck, prosperity |
| Gold (金) | Imperial Gold | `#C9A227` | Wealth, nobility |
| Ink (墨) | Deep Black | `#1A1A1A` | Calligraphy, knowledge |
| Rice Paper (宣纸) | Warm Beige | `#F5F0E6` | Tradition, heritage |
| Jade (玉) | Soft Green | `#2D6A4F` | Virtue, purity |

## 📖 Sample Proverbs

### Mandarin Proverbs

| Chinese | Pinyin | English |
|---------|--------|---------|
| 活到老，学到老 | huó dào lǎo, xué dào lǎo | Live until old, learn until old. |
| 一寸光阴一寸金 | yī cùn guāng yīn yī cùn jīn | An inch of time is an inch of gold. |
| 千里之行，始于足下 | qiān lǐ zhī xíng, shǐ yú zú xià | A journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step. |
| 有志者事竟成 | yǒu zhì zhě shì jìng chéng | Where there is a will, there is a way. |

### Cantonese Proverbs (粤语谚语)

| Chinese | Jyutping | English |
|---------|----------|---------|
| 十個茶壺九個蓋 | sahp go chàh wu gáu go goi | Ten teapots but only nine lids. |
| 牛唔飲水唔撳得牛低頭 | ngàuh mh yám séui mh gahm dāk ngàuh dài tàuh | You can't force a cow to drink. |
| 有咁大隻蛤乸隨街跳 | yáuh gam daaih jek gap nàah chèuih gāai tiu | No such thing as a free lunch. |
| 豬籠入水 | jyū lùhng yahp séui | Money flowing in from all directions. |
