## 🏮 About

This is a beautifully designed website featuring over 200 Chinese proverbs (谚语, *yànyǔ*), presented in both Mandarin Chinese and English. The design draws inspiration from ancient Chinese aesthetics, featuring traditional colors like vermilion red, imperial gold, and rice paper beige.

### Features

- 📚 **200+ Proverbs** - A comprehensive collection of timeless Chinese wisdom
- 🌏 **Bilingual** - Each proverb in Mandarin Chinese with English translation
- 🔍 **Search** - Find proverbs by keyword in either language
- 🏷️ **Categories** - Filter by Wisdom, Learning, Perseverance, Friendship, and Life
- 🎲 **Random Proverb** - Discover wisdom at random
- 📋 **Copy & Share** - Easily copy or share your favorite proverbs
- 📱 **Responsive** - Beautiful on all devices
- 🎨 **Authentic Design** - Ancient Chinese aesthetic with traditional colors

## 🎨 Design Philosophy

The website embraces traditional Chinese aesthetics:

| Element | Color | Hex |

| Rice Paper (宣纸) | Warm Beige | `#F5F0E6` |
